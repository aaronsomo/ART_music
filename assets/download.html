South Lake Tahoe’s SnowGlobe Music Festival announces NYE lineup

  • Staff report
  • September 17, 2019, 10:59 pm
  • facebook
  • twitter
  • stumbleupon

MTV today released the lineup for the annual SnowGlobe Music Festival, held in South Lake Tahoe.

The festival, which focuses on electronic and hip-hop acts, will include Skrillex, Fisher, Gigantic NGHTMRE, Griz, E-40, Louis the Child, Tchami x Malaa, Vince Staples, A-Trak, Earthgang and others over three days leading up to New Year’s Eve.

Other entertainment will again include skiing and snowboarding demonstrations alongside music performances. The festival, now in its ninth year and the largest music event held over the New Year’s Eve holiday, is close to ski resorts and casinos, and will offer several afterparties. It will again be punctuated by a fireworks display.

Tickets are available here. See below for the full lineup.
